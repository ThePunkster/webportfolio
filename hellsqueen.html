Hi! I did a swift project on my "to-do list" from my character design period at MST School.

This time we had to design a character based on our personal favorite characters. I choose 9 of them: Katherine Howard from Six, Asami Sato from Avatar: The Legend of Korra, Elsa from Frozen, Loba from Apex Legends, Lillian Vass from Magic, Shego from Kim Possible, Purgatori from Dynamite, Carmilla from Castlevania, and Nadia from Arcana.

It was a really fun project to do!
