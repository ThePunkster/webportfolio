June 4, 2023

A concept for Loba, a beloved character from Apex Legends. Drawing inspiration from her name, which means "wolf" in Spanish, I envisioned a remarkable skin that truly reflects her essence. This unique design portrays Loba as a wolf in sheep's clothing, skillfully blending danger with allure. Unassuming at first glance, this captivating persona holds a hidden power, ready to strike at any moment.

Crafted with passion and ingenuity, this wolf-in-sheep's-clothing skin epitomizes the art of character design. It showcases the fusion of contrasting traits, seamlessly combining danger and allure, making Loba an enigmatic and formidable figure. As an aspiring character designer, I take pride in bringing imagination to life through visual storytelling. I eagerly await the opportunity to contribute my artistic prowess to further elevate the captivating world of Apex Legends. Thank you for considering my work, and I am excited about the prospect of joining your esteemed team to create more thrilling experiences together.
