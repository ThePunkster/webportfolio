As a personal project, I wanted to create a skin for Catalyst from the game Apex Legends. Catalyst is one of my favorite characters in the franchise, and I wanted to create a skin for her simply to practice my conceptual art skills and take my time with the process. The concept is "succubus witch tech" and I wanted to represent that in her skin. I used natural textures that emulate dry branches for the witch part, horns, tails, and colors that represent the succubus. Since Apex is a battle royale game, it obviously had to have armor that follows Catalyst's signature shapes, but alters them enough to give it a new feel. I'm not only working on this skin, but also on a concept for a supposed prestige skin. Overall, a very fun project to make, especially the digital painting part as always.
