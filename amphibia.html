Project completed for MST School during the environmental semester, featuring a mining town for Disney's animated series Amphibia. With this project, I stepped completely out of my comfort zone. Despite having many personal matters to attend to, I am pleased to see that I was able to complete and deliver this piece on time, with the expected level of quality.
