Sep 30, 2021

Presenting a captivating concept design inspired by the intriguing Pentaped from "Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them." This rendition stays true to the creature's original story while infusing it with an even more ominous and chilling appearance. Imbued with a passion for storytelling and a keen eye for detail, I sought to bring forth a creature that elicits awe and fear, leaving an indelible impression on audiences. With a commitment to excellence and a desire to contribute to extraordinary projects, I eagerly anticipate the opportunity to collaborate and unleash captivating narratives through my artistic prowess.
